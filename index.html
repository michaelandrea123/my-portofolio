About Me

Hello! I’m Michael Andrea, a passionate full-stack web developer and graphic designer dedicated to crafting user-friendly digital experiences. I have a deep interest in both design and development, and I continuously strive to advance my skills while keeping up with the latest trends and technologies in the web development world. My expertise spans across the entire development spectrum—from designing intuitive interfaces to building robust server-side applications. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, GitLab, and Laravel, I’ve had the privilege to work on various web projects, including an enriching internship at Medicord where I learned and grew within a professional environment. I also enjoy leveraging design tools like Figma to transform creative ideas into visually engaging products. My goal is to continually expand my knowledge in full-stack development and design, contributing to projects that make a meaningful impact. When I'm not coding or designing, you'll likely find me exploring emerging tech trends or working on my own creative endeavors. Feel free to explore my portfolio, and let’s connect if you’re interested in collaborating on your next project!
